Seeking volunteers for an interview study

In this study, we are trying to understand how Tor users interact with onion services. As part of our project we are looking for Tor users to interview about how they use Tor and onion services. An interview will take 30–60 minutes. While we prefer to interview people in person at the Citizen Lab Summer Institute 2017, we are also able to do interviews over the Internet. To be eligible, you must be 18 years or older and you must have used Tor Browser (and ideally onion services) in the past. We are particularly interested in interviewing non-expert users. To compensate you for your time, we can offer you an Amazon.com gift card worth 20 USD.

To volunteer, please fill out this Google form. If selected, we will contact you to schedule a time and place for the interview.
