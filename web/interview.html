Seeking volunteers for an interview study

In this study, we are trying to understand how Tor users interact with onion services. You can learn more about our research on our project page. As part of our study, we are conducting a series of interviews with Tor users to understand how they use Tor and onion services. The interview will take approximately 30–60 minutes. To be eligible, you must be 18 years or older and you must have used Tor Browser (and ideally onion services) in the past. We are particularly interested in interviewing non-expert users. To compensate you for your time, we can offer you a Visa gift card or an Amazon.com gift card worth 20 USD.

To participate, please fill out this Google form. If selected, we will contact you to schedule a time and place for the interview.
